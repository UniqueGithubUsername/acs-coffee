Get started.


If using for the first time, you have to request the link to your profile through the Home page

You will receive an email with your personal access link. Using the link will bring you to your user profile.

To add a cup to your user profile, use Add a cup located in your profile as shown in the picture below.

Your link will have a personal suffix, which can be in two ways:

Accessing profile without adding cup: http://137.226.248.61:31387/user/your-suffix

Adding a cup directly to user profile: http://137.226.248.61:31387/add/your-suffix

The main reasons for making the coffee management an online service are reducing the time and effort for coffee administration, not having to print the coffee list, less manual work for accountings, bills can be send per email and new people can be added onto the list by everyone.

Enjoy your coffees :)
